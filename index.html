Expert Insights

What is an Apostille?
An Apostille is an official certificate issued by the government to verify the authenticity of a signature on a document for international use.
How long does legalisation take?
Timelines range from 24 hours on our Express service to 5 working days for our Standard service.
Do you accept original documents?
Yes, most authorities require the original physical document to be certified, although some can be processed as notarised copies.
